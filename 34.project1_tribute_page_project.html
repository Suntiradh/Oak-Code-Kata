The man who kept many times

Mr. Pravit Wongsawan The person who invented the science of keeping time, by the law of time cannot do anything for him.

Here's a time line of Mr.Pravit's life:

  • 1942 - Born in Nang Non Cave
  • 1990 - He began to wonder why people are 24 hours a day. Therefore began to search for the answer.
  • 1995 - Graduated form Hogwarts and Joined the National Security Council of Thailand
  • 1999 - He began to lead the team to grab time aside from normal.
  • 2010 - in the end, his search was successful. He discovered the "Eye of Akamotto" and began to learn to use it.
  • 2015 - He began to have a passion for spending time. Until greed began to overwhelm his mind and led him to do the bad thing, that is to snatch the time from his friends.
  • 2020 - When to take a lot more than a normal person would be available, the side effects are caused him to fall asleep without realizing it sometimes works and begin to have health problems.
  • 2022 - He in the future, going back in time, told him that in the next 2 years, he would die because of possession and spend too much time (of others) and suggest that he return every minute to the person he took.
  • 2024 - dies at the age of 82.Because he doesn't believe in his advice in the future.
Throughout his life He did things that no human had ever done before. History may engrave his name if what he does is not wrong. His life will be a lesson for future generations to know, "Don't play with time."
